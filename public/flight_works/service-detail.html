This wide body aircraft boasts not only of an impressive cabin but range that is second to none. The Challenger 605 aircraft seats up to 9 passengers comfortably for coast to coast or international trips. With a range of over 7 hours, the aircraft can go from anywhere inside of the continental United States and to many Western Europe destinations nonstop. The full galley offers modern conveniences to prepare gourmet in-flight meals. A Cabin Manager is provided to ensure each passenger experiences exceptional in-flight service.
